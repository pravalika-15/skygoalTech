How We Process?

Before starting any project, we follow these 6 steps
Understanding the objective

In order to develop fully functional project, we first understand the objectives/requirements of our customers. This is the most important concern we make, before we begin planning and working on the entire development process.

Planning

To get started, you must first plan. Half the battle is won if a strategy is devised. We can't predict the project's stability or susceptibility until we have a strategy in place. The project begins with planning, and the quality of that planning impacts the project's progress.

Design

Following analysis, designing takes control. It is essentially constructing the project's architectural structure. These procedures guarantee that any faults or defects are eliminated. In addition, standards and requirements might be established in order to adhere to them while building software.

Development & Implementation

In this step project development and implementation process actually starts. And, when the product is being produced, it is subjected to a pilot study to see whether or not it is performing properly.

Testing

After the project is developed, it goes through several stages of testing. This determines whether or not the product established is original. Any bugs or malfunctions discovered at this period can be rectified.

Maintenance

Last but not least, the project needs extensive maintenance both before and after it is delivered to the user. Before delivering the project to the customer/clients, the developer team ensures that it is in good working order and provides after-sales service support and assistance with the product's operation.
