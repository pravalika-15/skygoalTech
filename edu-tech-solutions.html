Edu-Tech Solutions

Website

Websites provide your institute with a global presence, and they are an essential strategy to produce more attention for your institution - whether that means more students or just prospective students who are geographically distant. By developing a website, we transform an archaic admission process into a digital one.

Hosting

We provide hosting solutions for your institute. Our hosting solutions can be scaled to fit your institute needs you have. Whether it is a private server, VPS, Dedicated server, or even just a domain hosting account, we have got you covered.

School/College ERP

A custom school/college ERP is the best way to give you the tools you need to manage the operations of your institution. The sky is the limit when it comes to what you can do with an integrated software program.

Digital Advertising

Targeting the right audience is our priority with Digital Advertising. Promoting your institute can be done in a number of ways, but you can only reach prospective students through one method. Don't wait until they decide where to study, reach out to them now with the help of our digital advertising services.

Support & Maintenance

We provide a monthly service that will help your website, content management systems (CMS's), and blogs run flawlessly. our Support & Maintenance experts will take care of everything else. We aim to help keep your business up and running.
